Spain

2020

BASIC INFO:

Duration

Start - 19th of July 2020

End - 30th of July 2020

Transport and locations

We flew by a plane to get there and back. We took a flight to Bordeaux in France and from there we used "BlaBlaBus" to get to Basque Country region in Spain.

We were in San Sebastian for 4 days, In Zarautz for 3 days, 1 day in Urkiola Natural Park - Alluitz Mountain (1034) and we also visited Bakio and spent some time in Bordeaux in France.

TRAVEL DESCRIPTION:

Day 1 -Our flight get to Bordeaux late night so we traveled only one kilometer from the aiprot to Ibis budget hotel where we sepnt our first night.

Day 2 -The next day we spent in Bordeaux, we visited center and had a walk across the city, in the early evening we took a bus to San Sebastian (it took around 4 hours from Bordequx)

Days 3 to 5 - Next three days we stayed in San Sebastian, where we surfed in the mornings (Zuriolla beach) and in the afternoons and evenings we explored the city monuments, tried different types of food, mostly "pintxos" and explore its famous places such as : Monte Urgull, Aquarium, La Parte Vieja, La Concha beach

Day 6 - On the 6th day we borrowed a car in San Sebastian and took a trip to Bakio and Gaztelugatxe (but unfortunetelly we were not able to visit the place, because we did not book tickets in advance). Later we get to Zarautz where we spent few next days.

Day 7 - This was probably the best day of the whole trip. We drove to Urkiola Natural Park in the morning to visit the mountains. We stayed there the whole day and we managed to get to the top of Alluitz Mountain (1034 meters above see level). We had not much drink with us and the "climb" was really hard but now it is a gret memory.

Day 8 and 9 - We were most of the time at Zarautz beach Surfing, but we also explored the Zarautz city and its restaurants (Pintxos again)

Day 10 and 11 - In those days we were slowly finisfhing our trip so we returned the borrowed car and stayed in San Sebastian over night. Than we took BlaBlaBus again and rode to Bordeaux where we stayed over night and next day before the flight back home, we visited ZOO, where we were nicely surprised by lots of free runs (lemurs for example)
