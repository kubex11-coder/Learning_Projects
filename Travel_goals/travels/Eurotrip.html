Eurotrip

2019

BASIC INFO:

Duration

Start - 2nd of September 2019

End - 14th of September 2019

Transport and locations

Three different locations, three different flights.

2nd of September - Flight to Marseille in France

4th of September - Flight to Porto in Portugal

11td of September - Flight to Barcelona in Spain

TRAVEL DESCRIPTION:

Day 1 -Our flight landed in Bordeaux late night so we traveled only one kilometer from the aiport to Ibis budget hotel, where we spent our first night.

Day 2 -The next day we spent in Bordeaux, we visited center and had a walk across the city, in the early evening we took a bus to San Sebastian (it took around 4 hours to get there from Bordeaux)

Days 3 to 5 - Next three days we stayed in San Sebastian, where we surfed in the mornings (Zuriolla beach) and in the afternoons and evenings we explored the city monuments, tried different types of food, mostly "pintxos" and explore its famous places such as : Monte Urgull, Aquarium, La Parte Vieja, La Concha beach

Day 6 - On the 6th day we borrowed a car in San Sebastian and took a trip to Bakio and Gaztelugatxe (but unfortunetelly we were not able to visit the place, because we did not book tickets in advance). Later we drove to Zarautz where we spent few next days.

Day 7 - This was probably the best day of the whole trip. We drove to Urkiola Natural Park in the morning to visit the mountains. We stayed there the whole day and we managed to get to the top of Alluitz Mountain (1034 meters above see level). We had not much drink with us and the "climb" was really exhausting and long, but now after some time it is a really great memory.

Day 8 and 9 - We were most of the time on Zarautz beach Surfing, but we also explored the Zarautz city and its restaurants (Pintxos again)

Day 10 and 11 - In those days we were slowly finisfhing our trip so we returned the borrowed car and stayed in San Sebastian over night. Than we took BlaBlaBus again and rode back to Bordeaux where we stayed over night and next day before the flight home, we visited ZOO, where we were nicely surprised by lots of animals and free runs (lemurs for example)
