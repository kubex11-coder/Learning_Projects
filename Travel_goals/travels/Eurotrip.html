Eurotrip

2019

BASIC INFO:

Duration

Start - 2nd of September 2019

End - 14th of September 2019

Transport and locations

Three different locations, three different flights.

2nd of September - Flight to Marseille in France

4th of September - Flight to Porto in Portugal

11th of September - Flight to Barcelona in Spain

TRAVEL DESCRIPTION:

Day 1 - Our flight landed in Marseille Airport around 5PM and we took a bus to Marseille train station. Our Airbnb accomodation was circa half hour walk from train station, but I am not going to lie. I really did not feel exactly comfortable walking in those streets in early evening. Maybe I was just scared of the bad reputation Marseille has in some articles.

Day 2 -This day we spent in Massif des Calanques which is located at the end of Marseille city. We get there by bus. Massif des Calanques was beautifull but we did not take enough water and it was really hot so we were little tired in the moment.

Days 3 - We had a little walk around the Old Port and city center in Marseille (the weather and atmosphere were really great that day) and in the evening we traveled back to the Marseille airport - from there we flew to Porto in Portugal

Day 4 - day 9 - Those days we spent in Porto, we had accomodation near Matosinhos beach where we spent few days surfing. One day we also went to surf to Leca de Palmeira beach where the waves were bigger and wilder. Other days when we were not surfing we traveled by metro (but it was not underground so it was more like a train) to the city center, where we walked around Douro river, Louis I. bridge and saw other Porto monuments.

Day 10 - Last day in Porto we visited Maia Zoo, where we spent few hours before we traveled to the airport (good thing is that it is possible to get from the city to the airport only by metro). From Porto airport we flew to our last travel destination which was Barcelona in Spain

Day 11 - This whole day we spent walking on Barcelona streets, we saw Park Guell, Sagrada de Familia, Tore des Glories and other Barcelona monuments.

Day 12 - The last day we took a 80km trip from Barcelona to Tossa de Mar where we found a nice spot for snorkeling. I was also able to find a restaurant which served very good spanish Paella for only 1 person (because my girlfriend did not like it and it was really hard to find back in Barcelona). So the last day was really nice and relaxed as it should be. The day later after we woke up, we headed to the airport and flew back home to Czech Republic.
