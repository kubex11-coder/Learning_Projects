Duration

Start - 11th of June 20201/p>

End - 21th of July 2020

Transport and locations

As a transport throughout the whole trip we used a car. Our trip started 11th of June at 2AM. We drove a car for 6 hours from Mladá Boleslav to Plansee. We drove in this direction - Plzeň, Rozvadov, Munchen (Germany) and Plansee

We were in Plansee Sennalpe camp for 3 days, In Innsbruck for 3 days and also 3 days in Gerlosberg (near Mayrhofen)

TRAVEL DESCRIPTION:

Day 1 -Our travel started at 2AM and we get to the Sennalpe camp around 8AM. We built the tent and we spent the day, sunbathing, swimming near the lake.

Day 2 -This day we drove circa 20km from the camp to visit Higline 179 and the forts nearby (Ehrenberg, Schlosskopf, Claudia)

Days 3 - We took a trip around the Plansse lake (around 20km) - very nice weather, very good hike. After the trip we moved to the Innsbruck.

Day 4 - During the day we visited Schaflegerkogel loop hike, which is 20km from Innsbruck and you have to pay 3Euros for the road to get there (and the road is really bad - my car cried during the ride there). The hike itself was really nice (but hard). After the hike we visited Alpenzoo in Innsbruck in the evening.

Day 5 - We borrowed bicycles and we rode the Innradweg which leads in two direction from Innsbruck along the Inn river. In the evening we visited Ambras castle.

Day 6 - We left Innsbruck and after 150km we arrived to Schlegeisspeicher - dam 1712 above sea. From there we took a hardest hike during the whole trip to Olpererhutte 2389 above sea. Also we have to pay 14Euros to be able to ride the road to Schlegeis dam, but at least the road was very good this time. In the evening we arrived to our Gerlosberg Airbnb.

Day 7 -This day was all about skiing. We drove to Hintertux 3200 above sea (40km from accomodation) where we tried summer skiing. I am not the good skiier and the snow was wet and heavy so it was really difficult. We gave it few hours and then headed back to our accomodation where we drank some beers and chilled.

Day 8 - Last day we took a trip to Krimmel waterfall (40km from accomodation) and after that we packed our stuff and get back home.
